Experiment 5 - 3D Graphics

Imitate

I started this experiment with no idea in mind. Instead I decided to browse various examples online until I found one I would like to reference. One example I came across was a series of generative chunks which would rotate around the screen and shift colors between them and the background.

Integrate

The first area I focused on was understanding the different uses of color in the sample. Once I did this, I was able to reflect the design using different colors of my choice, where the design would shift between these colors. Moving on to the chunks themselves, I read through the code to understand the methods used in the example. I started by changing the rotation values of each direction. Then, I modified the vertices of each chunk when rendered on the screen so that they would appear with a different structure from the sample. From here, I returned to the rotation properties, now changing the translation values instead. This segment would affect the direction each chunk moves when the design is in motion. I ended up coming across a result in which the chunks gave the illusion of orbiting around the center, which became my inspiration for enhancing the design.

Innovate

When it came to this step, my idea was to contain all the chunks within a large sphere to give the appearence of energy swirling inside a core. After accomplishing this, I looked into adding textures into my design. First, I added a simple texture to the chunks, followed by the sphere. However, the sphere did not apply the way I initially wanted, since it covered up the chunks. I experimented more with the sphere a little more, before converting it to another shape entirely. Now as a torus, I felt that it fit the theme of a design featuring orbiting objects. To complete my design, I added in more shapes to orbit around the chunks, applying textures to them as well and making each one a different size with their own rotations.

Reflection

In the past, I have not worked on much in terms of 3D graphics so this was my first time learning a lot more about them. In my experiment, I was given a small introduction to lighting, shaders, and textures, which I would like to continue studying in the future. Additionly, I appreciate how my design was able to turn out given my background on the subject. While I did not have an initial idea, I was able to come up with a theme by the end, which is well illustrated in the final design.
